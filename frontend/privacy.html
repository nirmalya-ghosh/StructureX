Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 4, 2026

Do not upload personal, confidential, restricted, classified, or regulated infrastructure data unless you have authority to process it and understand the risk.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how StructureX handles information in the browser-based command center, static deployment, and supporting API routes.

2. Information Processed

  • Account form details such as name and email entered on the login or signup screen.
  • Browser session data stored locally, including mock user session state and dashboard activity history.
  • Uploaded CSV files and scenario inputs used to generate analysis during a session.
  • Map coordinates, search queries, selected building metadata, weather targets, and generated risk outputs.
  • Technical logs and hosting metadata that may be processed by Vercel, browser providers, and external APIs.

3. How Information Is Used

  • To authenticate the demo session and personalize the dashboard.
  • To run scenario, building, resilience, satellite, and dataset analysis features.
  • To operate, secure, debug, improve, and monitor service performance.
  • To protect against misuse, unsafe reliance, abuse, fraud, or unauthorized access.

4. Local Storage and Cookies

StructureX uses browser storage for session state and dashboard activity. You can clear it by signing out, using in-app clear actions, or clearing browser site data. Hosting providers and external services may use cookies or similar technologies for security, analytics, and service delivery.

5. Third-Party Services

StructureX may connect to services such as Vercel, MapTiler, Open-Meteo, Nominatim/OpenStreetMap, Google-hosted assets, Font Awesome, and CDN providers. Those services may receive request metadata such as IP address, user agent, referrer, coordinates or queries sent to their APIs, and timing data.

6. Sensitive Data Restrictions

  • Do not upload personal data, health data, financial data, credentials, government identifiers, security-sensitive infrastructure details, or confidential files unless legally authorized.
  • Do not enter information that could expose critical infrastructure vulnerabilities, private individuals, protected locations, or non-public emergency plans.
  • You are responsible for redaction, consent, legal basis, retention rules, and compliance obligations for uploaded or entered data.

7. Security

StructureX uses reasonable technical and organizational measures for a portfolio/demo deployment, but no internet service is perfectly secure. You must not rely on StructureX as the sole repository for critical records or safety decisions.

8. Retention

Browser-local data remains until cleared by the user or browser. Serverless request data is processed to return responses and may be subject to hosting provider logs, retention settings, and security monitoring.

9. Your Choices

  • Use the app without uploading sensitive data.
  • Clear browser storage and sign out when finished.
  • Review third-party service policies before using map, weather, or CDN-backed features.
  • Contact the project maintainer through GitHub for privacy or security concerns.

10. Changes

This policy may be updated as StructureX changes. Continued use after updates means you acknowledge the revised policy.
